Monsters

An adventure without adversaries would be boring, and flavorless without the fun kind of monstrous creature's we've all come to enjoy in our fantasy role playing games. Here I will give some examples of monsters, but I always encourage the creation of custom monsters within a group to make things more personal and less predictable. See monster creation for suggestions on how to build monsters.

Inferentity, Fire MK I

(Small Elemental)

A fire inferentity looks vaguely like a reflection of its creator, except made entirely out of coals, flames, and other heat effects. In its hand is a ghostly tulwar seemingly drawn from nothing. While not incorporeal these creatures can certainly look like they are.

Hit Dice: 2d8+10 (22 hp)
Initiative: +4
Movement: 20 ft, 40 ft Fly (Perfect)
Armor Class: 17 (+1 Parry, +1 Natural, +4 Dex, +1 Size)
Base Attack/Grapple: +1/-1
Attack: Melee +8 Tulwar (1d6+3 18-20 x2) or Melee +11 Tulwar Energy Charge (1d6+3+1d6 [Heat] 18-20 x2)
Full Attack: Melee +8 Tulwar or Melee +11 Tulwar Energy Charge
Space/Reach: 5 ft. / 5 ft.
Abilities: Lesser Ephemeral Weapon, Energy Charge (Heat)
Special Qualities: Heat Absorbing
Saves: Fort +2, Ref +7, Will +6
Attributes: Str 14, Dex 18, Con 14, Int 14, Wis 16, Cha 16
Skills: Acrobatic +7, Balance +7, Concentration +7, Escape Artist +9, Intimidate +6, Knowledge (The Planes) +5, Intimidate +6, Sleight of Hand +7
Feats: Weapon Fixation (One Handed: Curved Sword) and Weapon Defensive Training (One Handed: Curved Sword)
Environment: Plane of Fire, summoned by mage
Organization: Typically solitary
Challenge Rating: CR 1
Possessions: None
Spell/Attack Curve: Non-Magic, Generalist, Medium Attack
Definitions: Elemental, Corporeal, Heat

Heat Absorbing(Ex) Any heat damage will only heal a fire inferentity and cannot deal damage to it.

Energy Charge(Su)
Energy charge is usable 5 times a day as a swift action and lasts for 1 round.

When charged the inferentity gains +1d6 heat damage to all of its attacks as well as it's charisma modifier (+3) to all of its attack for the duration.

Lesser Ephemeral Weapon(Su) As a swift action an inferentity can summon its weapon of choice. This weapon is considered to have +1 enhancement.
