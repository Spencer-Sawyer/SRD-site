Compulsion Spells

Compulsion spells can affect the mind and thinking of another creature. Sometimes in beneficial ways, other times in negative ways. Mind control and suggestions are the most famous types of compulsion spells. Spells that instill divine fervor, or battle trances are also considered compulsion.
