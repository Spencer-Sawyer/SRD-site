Lesser Breath Weapon (Ex)

Prerequisite: Constitution 14, Draconic Bloodline
Use: 1 + Con mod per day; only every 1d4 rounds.
Action: Standard action
Target: 30-ft Cone or 60-ft Line, chosen upon taking this ability.
Duration: Instant
Saving Throw: Reflex Half
DC: Constitution Based
Spell Resistance: No

Gain a breath weapon of your Draconic Bloodline energy type. This will deal 2d4 damage and will increase by 2d4 per 3 HD.

Alternatively instead of dealing damage, a status breath weapon can be taken, such as sleep, nauseated, stunned, etc..., that will last for 1d6 rounds. Further iterations of this ability will not extend the duration, only the range. Note this may change the save effect to will or fortitude depending on the status effect. For example a sleep gas will be fortitude based, while a fear breath weapon will be will based.
