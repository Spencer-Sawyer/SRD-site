Greater Aura (Ex)

Prerequisite:Improved Aura, Charisma 18, HD 12+
Use: 1 + Cha Mod per day
Action: Swift action
Effect: 30 ft radius centered on the user
Duration: 1 Hour/HD

This functions as Lesser Aura but the bonuses have changed.

Resistance: Gain 35 Resistance to any one of the following energy types; Fire, Ice, Electricity, Acid, Sonic or Force.

Fast Healing: All allies within the aura heal 15 HP per round, this will stabilize any dying allies.

Poise: Allies within the aura gain a +3 luck bonus to initiative and +8 luck bonus to Spot and Listen.

Damage Reduction: Allies gain 12/- Damage Reduction. Note this does not stack with any other form of damage reduction.

Saver: Gain a +5 luck bonus to any one of the following; Will, Fortitude, Reflex.
