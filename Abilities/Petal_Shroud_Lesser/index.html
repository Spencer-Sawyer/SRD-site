Lesser Petal Shroud (Su)

Prerequisite: PMA 14+
Use: Can be broken up as needed but total use per day cannot exceed maximum duration.
Action: Standard
Target: Self
Duration: 1 minute per HD.

When activated a shroud of flower petals, leaves, or whatever chosen plant material works surrounds the caster and becomes a sort of armor. This grants +1 natural armor, which increases by 1 for every 4 HD. It also grants 2 natural vine swipes that have +5 ft of reach. These two attacks are at your highest base attack bonus and use their PMA as the modifier instead of using your strength or dexterity modifier. Damage is 1d6 + PMA mod with a x2 critical.
