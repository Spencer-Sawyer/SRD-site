Natural Magic (Su)

Prerequisite: PMA of at least 10 + base spell level
Use: 1 per day per primary mental attribute modifier
Action: Depends on Base Spell
Target: Depends on Base Spell
Duration: Depends on Base Spell
Saving Throw: Depends on Base Spell
Dispel: Depends on Base Spell
DC: PMA based
Spell Resistance: Depends on Base Spell

Select any spell from any spell list level 5 or less and gain it as a supernatural ability. The DC for this spell will be 10 + 1/2 HD + PMA modifier. This ability can be selected multiple times, the Uses per day are shared across all Natural Magic. Your character's HD is used as their caster level for purposes of duration, spell resistance, range and other CL dependent things.
