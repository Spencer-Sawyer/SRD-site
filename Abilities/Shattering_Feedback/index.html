Shattering Feedback (Ex)

Prerequisite: Constitution 20, Endure
Use: shared with Endure
Range: Personal
Target: Self
Action: Immediate
Duration: 1 round, 5 rounds, or 10 rounds; see text

All damage absorbed by the damage reduction from Endure is dealt back to the weapon that damaged it. When triggered attackers will have to make a reflex save DC equal to 10 + 1/2 HD + constitution modifier. If the attacker fails the save, the weapon takes the flat damage; hardness applies like normal.
