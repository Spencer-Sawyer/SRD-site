Flight of the Phoenix (Su)

Prerequisite: Charisma 14, Shroud of the Phoenix, cannot be undead, and must have the Blessing of the Phoenix
Use: 1 per day per PMA Mod
Action: Standard
Target: Selected targets
Duration: 1 hour/HD
Saving Throw: None

Gives your character flight of 60 ft (Perfect). Wings take on the appearance of the energy type.

Synergy: While moving with these wings all quills can be thrown as a free action with the same or different targets.
