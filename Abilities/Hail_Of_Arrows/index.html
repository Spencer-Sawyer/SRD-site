Hail of Arrows (Ex)

Prerequisite: Proficiency with weapon, must be using a bow type weapon.
Use: Dexterity mod per day
Action: Standard action
Target: 10-ft radius center on a target
Duration: Instant

Make an attack at your full attack bonus on a selected target. If there are more targets within 10-ft of the target your character can choose to target them as well at a -2 attack bonus. This must be decided before the first arrow is fired. The maximum number of creatures that can be affected is equal to your dexterity modifier +1. This action does not provoke an attack of opportunity.
