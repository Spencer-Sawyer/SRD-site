Lay on Hands

Prerequisite: PMA 16+
Use: ((2 x HD) x PMA Modifier)) of usable points for healing/damage.
Range: Touch
Target: Self
Action: Standard
Duration: Instantaneous
Saving Throw: Will
DC: PMA Based

Channel your energy to damage or heal your target by any amount up to the maximum amount within your point pool. If Lay on Hands is used to deal damage, the subject will get a will save for half damage. Points can also be spent to do other things. 25 points can be spent to, instead of healing, close a wound. 100 points can be spent to remove an injury, disease, or poison. 200 points can be used to remove a curse. Likewise, instead of dealing damage they could used to bestow the aforementioned effects instead. The subject will get a will save against this as well.
