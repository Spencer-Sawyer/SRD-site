Endure (Ex)

Prerequisite: Con 16
Use: 1 + 1 per 4 HD per day
Range: Personal
Target: Self
Action: Immediate action
Duration: 1 round, 5 rounds, or 10 rounds; see text.

Gain damage reduction for a brief period of time. Choose the duration of Endure before the ability is activated. 1 round will gain /- DR equal to 10 times your character's con mod, 5 rounds will be 3 times your character's con mod, and 10 rounds will be equal to your character's con mod.
