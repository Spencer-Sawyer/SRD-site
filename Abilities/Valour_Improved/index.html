Improved Valor (Ex)

Prerequisite: Must not have sneak attack, Strength 16 or Constitution 14
Range: Personal
Target: Self
Duration: Constant

As Valor but, the dodge and attack bonuses are increased to +3. You get an attack of opportunity any time opportunist is used against you, so long as you have any attacks of opportunity remaining. Non-touch spells cast in this position do not provoke attacks of opportunity.
