Lesser Opportunist (Ex)

Prerequisite: Dexterity 16 and move silently 4, Conceal 4 or Beguile 4, cannot have a valor feat
Action: Attack

Gain +1d6 damage per 2 HD (Max +3d6) with whatever attack was made against a sufficiently disadvantaged foe. This includes flanked foes, foes that are flat footed, or helpless foes. This will apply to multiple attacks. Whatever the primary damage type is, is the type of damage Opportunist gives. If this is done with a piercing weapon the bonus damage is piercing. With a touch spell it becomes whatever primary damage type the spell is e.g. fire damage.
