Inferentity

A common creature often summoned by conjurers to do their bidding. They are partially created from the mind of the caster. When summoned by a magic user they are intelligent creatures acting with initiative and intelligence borrowed from the caster. They all contain knowledge of the planes that can be of assistance. They always speak the root language of the caster that summoned it. If these are encountered on their home plane and not summoned by a magic user, they are animalistic in intelligence and initiative.

There are several types of inferentity depending on which planes they come from, and mixed variations could exist. The common inferentity are Caustic, Decay, Fire, Frost, Tempest, and Vital.

Inferentity, Caustic MK I

(Small Elemental)

An acid inferentity looks like its caster but formed out of liquid. This clear elemental emits sickening fumes and red colored gas as it moves.

Initiative: +4
Movement: 20 ft
Armor Class: 17 (+4 Dex, +2 Natural, +1 Size)
Base Attack/Grapple: +1/+0
Attack: Melee +8 Whip (1d3+6 x2) or Melee Whip +11 Energy Charge (1d3+6+1d6 [Acid] x2)
Full Attack: Melee +8 or Melee +11 Energy Charge
Space/Reach: 5 ft. / 10 ft.
Abilities: Lesser Ephemeral Weapon, Energy Charge (Acid)
Special Qualities: Acid Absorbing
Saves: Fort +5, Ref +7, Will +2
Attributes: Str 16, Dex 18, Con 14, Int 14, Wis 14, Cha 16
Skills: Acrobatic +8, Balance +7, Concentration +7, Escape Artist +7, Intimidate +7, Knowledge (The Planes) +7, Sleight of Hand +7, Spot +7
Feats: Weapon Fixation (Whip), Offensive Weapon Training (Whip) Environment: Plane of Water, summoned by mage
Organization: Typically solitary
Difficulty Level: DL 2
Possessions: None
Spell/Attack Curve: Non-Magic, Generalist, Medium Attack
Definitions: Elemental, Corporeal, acid

Acid Absorbing(Ex)
Any acid damage will only heal a Tempest inferentity and cannot deal damage to it.

Energy Charge(Su)
Energy charge is usable 5 times a day as a swift action and lasts for 1 round.

When charged the inferentity gains +1d6 acid damage to all of its attacks as well as it's charisma modifier (+3) to all of its attack for the duration.

Lesser Ephemeral Weapon(Su)
As a swift action an inferentity can summon its weapon of choice. This weapon is considered to have +1 enhancement.

Saver: Gain a +1 luck bonus to any one of the following; Will, Fortitude, Reflex.
