Conceal

Being able to hide objects, others, and yourself is sometimes considered the most important things to a character. Being out of sight and out of mind can give a lot of advantages. This is a relatively simple skill, conceal rolls are countered by spot rolls. There are some situations and details that can modify a roll. Please see the chart below for some basic modifiers on concealing. Anything concealing must not be in a bare room, there must be objects or structures to hide near.

Circumstance Roll Modifier
Tiny creature +16
Very Small Creature +8
Small Creature +4
Large Creature -4
Very Large Creature -8
Huge Creature -16
In dim light +3
In Darkness +6
In bright light -3
While being intensely observed -15
General vision obstruction +6
