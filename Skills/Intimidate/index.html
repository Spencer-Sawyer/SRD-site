Intimidate

Sometimes the best way to get what you want is to force your will another another via fear, a sense of danger, or other things that might make your target think twice on how to treat you. This skill is countered by a level check. The opponent of your intimidation check rolls 1d20 + HD + Will Save Total. If the subject fails they become scared. There are things that can modify your roll against your opponent's save. This list is by no means exhaustive and serves only as an example of what a DM can use to modify your roll.

Circumstance Roll Modifier
User is notably shorter than target -5
User is notable taller than target +5
User has a violent reputation +5
User has pacifistic reputation -5
User is associated with powerful people +8
