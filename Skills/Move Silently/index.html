Move Silently

The element of surprise, sneaking through a restricted area, or scaring your friends. This skill allows your character to move undetected by other creature's ears. The opposing roll to this is listen. There are a few things that can modify your roll.

Situation Roll Modifier
Soft ground with no clutter +5
Hard ground with no clutter +0
cluttered ground -2
Moving at 1/4 speed +2
Moving at 1/2 speed +0
Moving at full speed -5
Running -15
