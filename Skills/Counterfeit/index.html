Counterfeit

Being able to forge documents is one of the more useful skills in any urban environment, especially a bureaucratic one. Counterfeit is countered by a spot check, though there are things that modify your roll when forging some kind of document. Please refer to the table below to know what those modifiers are. Most of these examples apply the same to signatures. Counterfeiting with money is always difficult, as financial interests tend to have far, far more complex methods of preventing fakes.

Circumstance Roll Modifier
Counterfeiting with no knowledge -30
Counterfeiting with basic knowledge -20
Counterfeiting with examples of similar examples -5
Counterfeiting with an exact example +5
Counterfeiting with exact, but blank, document +10
Counterfeiting anything financial -50
