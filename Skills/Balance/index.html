Balance

There are many times when a character can easily lose their footing and fall prone. Other uses of balance would be walking on very thin surfaces or ledges. DCs for most balance checks are set by the spell or ability. Below is a chart for more static examples of balance DCs

Action Attempted DC
Walking on a slipper floor 12
Walking on icy floor 18
Walking on and width span 15
