Magical Items

Magical items are the second half of a character. Every encounter in the examples are built with a character having their level's gold worth of magical items. A character without their tools is vulnerable, especially at higher levels.

Armor/Shield Enhancement Costs

Armor enhancement is a bonus that increases the armor's hardness, hit points, and armor enhancement bonus. The price of this is determined by squaring the bonus and multiplying by two hundred. (Bonus2 x 200) Every enhancement bonus increases the hardness of an armor by 2 and the HP of the armor by 10. Enhancement costs for shields are the same, as are the hardness and HP bonuses.

Cost in Gold Enhancement Modifier
200 +1
800 +2
1,800 +3
3,200 +4
5,000 +5

Imbuements

On top of enhancement an armor can have a set number of imbuements. These are broken down into three categories, Lesser, Moderate, and Greater. The amount of imbuements on the weapon is limited by the enhancement modifier on the weapon. If a weapon is +5 it has a capacity of 5.
