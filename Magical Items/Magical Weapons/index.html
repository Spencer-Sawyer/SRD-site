Magical Weapons

Magical weapons are iconic and often held as the most important aspect of many a hero's arsenal. Many people dream of having their flaming sword or holy avenger. All magical weapons must be masterwork before that can be enhanced or imbued. Weapon enhancements cost (Bonus2 x 400) to a limit of +5.
