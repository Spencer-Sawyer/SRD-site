Weapon Mastery

Prerequisite: 10 HD, Improved Offensive Weapon Training, Defensive Weapon Training, and Improved Weapon Fixation

Benefit: With your chosen weapon gain another +1 to attack, +2 to damage, and +1 parry bonus to AC; that stacks with all other similar feats.

Special: This feat can only be taken on once.
