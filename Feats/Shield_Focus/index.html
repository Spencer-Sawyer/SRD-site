Shield Focus

Prerequisite: Proficient with your shield

Benefit: All shields gain +1 training bonus to AC and to hit with shield bash.

Bucklers: Gain stun on successful shield bash. If a shield bash does more than 15 damage the opponent must make a fortitude save against DC 10 + 1/2 HD + Strength Mod or be stunned for one round.

Full Shields: Gain 5 ft shove on any shield bash that does more than 15 damage.

Tower Shield: The tower shield itself, not the wielder, gains 15 energy resistance against all energy types except entropic.
