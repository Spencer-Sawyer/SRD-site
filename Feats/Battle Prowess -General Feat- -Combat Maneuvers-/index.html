Battle Prowess [General Feat] [Combat Maneuvers]

Prerequisite: Str 14, Dex 14, Wis 10

Benefit: Add your character's dex mod to the maximum number of total attacks of opportunity that can be made in a round. For example, if your character has a Dexterity of 14, then they would be able to make a total of 3 attacks of opportunity in a round.

Normal: A character/creature can only make one attack of opportunity in a round.
