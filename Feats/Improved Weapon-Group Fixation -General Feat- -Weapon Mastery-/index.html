Improved Weapon-Group Fixation [General Feat] [Weapon Mastery]

Prerequisite: HD 4, Weapon Group Fixation

Benefit: Gain another +1 to attacks with all weapons within the weapon group

Special: This stacks with Weapon Group Fixation. Unlike Weapon Group Fixation, this does stack with the Weapon Fixation line of feats.
