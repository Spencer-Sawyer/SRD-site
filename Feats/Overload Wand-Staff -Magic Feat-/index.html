Overload Wand/Staff [Magic Feat]

Prerequisite: None

Benefit: You can overload a wand to deal maximize damage with it, but uses five charges instead of one.

Special: When using this with use magic device failing the activation DC by 5 or more will cause the spell to backfire on you, maximized.
