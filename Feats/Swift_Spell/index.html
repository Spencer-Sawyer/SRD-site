Swift Spell

Prerequisite: Must be standard or advanced caster.

Benefit: Can use known spells as a swift action, or prepare a swift spell, so long as it's casting time is standard. Any full-round action spell is reduced to standard action.. This take a spell slot four levels higher than the spell it is being used on. So a swift Shield spell will take a fifth level spell slot.
