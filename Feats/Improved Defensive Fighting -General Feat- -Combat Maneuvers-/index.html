Improved Defensive Fighting [General Feat] [Combat Maneuvers]

Prerequisite: Intelligence 13

Benefit: A character, at the beginning of their turn, can take anywhere between -1 to -5 on their attack, and gain a +1 to +5 dodge bonus to their AC in exchange.
