Mixed Metamagic [Magic Feat]

Prerequisite: HD 9+

Benefit: Your character and mix two of the following metamagic feats, Enlarge, Extend, Silent, or Still; together. This will add the slot requirements together resulting in the spell requiring a slot 2 levels higher to cast.
