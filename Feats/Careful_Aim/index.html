Careful Aim

Prerequisite: Dex 14

Benefit: When firing into squares occupied by two or more entities, your character no longer has a chance to hit other creatures in the square. Firing through occupied squares no longer provides cover to further targets.

Normal: When firing into squares occupied by two or more entities, you have a % chance of hitting the wrong target. When firing through occupied squares, the further targets would gain +4 cover bonus to AC.
