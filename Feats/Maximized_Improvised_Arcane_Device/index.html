Maximize Improvised Arcane Device

Prerequisite: Improved Arcane Device, ability to cast 4th level spells

Benefit: A damage effect from an improvised arcane device can be maximized, but it takes 3 spell levels higher to do so. For example to maximize an effect that sacrifices a level 4 spell to deal the appropriate damage for that level, a 7th level spell slot will need to be used.
