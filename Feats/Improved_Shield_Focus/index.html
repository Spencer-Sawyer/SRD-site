Improved Shield Focus

Prerequisite: Proficient with your shield

Benefit: All shields gain an additional +1 training bonus to AC and to hit with shield bash.

Bucklers: Gain +2 base damage on shield bash.

Full Shields: Shove gains another 5 ft. of push

Tower Shield: The resistance bonus is increased to 25 for all energy types except entropic.
