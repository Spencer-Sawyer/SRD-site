Improved Weapon-Group Fixation

Prerequisite: HD 4 and Weapon Group Fixation.

Benefit: Gain another +1 to attacks with all weapons within the weapon group

Special: This stacks with Weapon Group Fixation. Unlike Weapon Group Fixation, this does stack with the Weapon Mastery line of feats.
