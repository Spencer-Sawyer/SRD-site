Bolster Breath Weapon

Prerequisite: HD 12+

Benefit: Breath weapons gain an extra 50% damage dice and this feature is usable once per day per 10 HD. If a breath weapon would do 10d6, when bolstered, it would instead do 15d6. This adds 3 rounds to the 1d4 round cool down between breaths.
