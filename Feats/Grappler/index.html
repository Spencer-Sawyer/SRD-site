Grappler

Prerequisite: 5 HD, Lethal Unarmed Strike, Unarmed Combatant definition, or your unarmed attacks are naturally lethal

Benefit: When making the initial touch attack to initiate a grapple you no longer provoke an attack of opportunity from your opponent. Also gain a +4 on all grapple checks, whether or not you initiated the grapple.
