Deadly Accuracy

Prerequisite: 8 HD and trained with the weapon

Benefit: The critical range of a selected weapon is doubled. So a weapon that is normally in critical threat a 20 is rolled will become 19-20. Likewise a weapon that is 19-20 will become 17-20 which is double the chance. A weapon that is 18-20 will become 15-20. As is typical with critical hits these still need to be confirmed like normal.

Special: This feat can be taken multiple times, each time it is taken it will apply to a new weapon.
