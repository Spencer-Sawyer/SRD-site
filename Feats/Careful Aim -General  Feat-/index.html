Careful Aim [General Feat]

Prerequisite: Dex 14

Benefit: When firing into squares occupied by two or more entities, your character no longer has a chance to hit other creatures in the square.

Normal: When firing into squares occupied by two or more entities, you have a % chance of hitting the wrong target.
